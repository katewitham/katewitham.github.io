About this Flare Project

This website, built using MadCap Flare, discusses accessibility in the digital age and provides information about numerous different digital accessibility tools. Each section provides background and significance for each tool along with "How To" sections explaining how to implement each tool into ClipChamp, a video editing software.

Section 1: Introduction to Accessibility

The introduction to accessibility secion of this website discusses the importance of considering accessibility while developing web content. It draws on readings from Laura Kalbag and Adobe to emphasize the importance of accessibility and inclusive design. There are three subsections:

  • What is a Disability?

  • Universal vs. Accessible Design

  • Why does Accessibility Matter?

Collaborators - Kate Witham

Section 2: Video Transcripts

The video transcripts section of this website explains the significance of transcripts including what types of disabilities they help with most along with guidance on best practices and a section on how to generate and add transcripts to videos on ClipChamp.The subsections include:

  • What are Transcripts?

  • How to Edit Transcripts

  • How to create Transcripts in ClipChamp

  • Best Practices

Collaborators - Jessica, Meghan, Kalana

Section 3: Closed Captions

This section dicusses the history and significance of Closed Captions as well as tutorials on how to create and implement them into ClipChamp content. The subsections include:

  • What are Closed Captions?

  • History of Closed Captions

  • Editing Closed Captions for a ClipChamp Video

  • Adding Closed Captions to a ClipChamp Video

Collaborators - Jane, Zoe, Dylan

Section 4: Audio Description

The audio description section includes information on the importance of audio descriptions as well as tutorials on how to record and add audio descriptions to a video in ClipChamp. The subsections include:

  • What is Audio Description?

  • How to Record a Tutorial or Demo that uses Integrated Description

Section 5: Chapter Cards/Visual Formatting

This section on chapter cards not only explains their role and significance in the world of digital accessibilty, but it also provides step-by-step walkthroughs that teach users how to create and edit chapter cards in ClipChamp. The subsections are:
