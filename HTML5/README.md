# Overview of MadCap Flare Project!
---
This website, built using *MadCap Flare*, discusses accessibility in the digital age. As of right now, it includes a section introducing accessibility and another section showing users how to create chapter cards in ClipChamp. 

## About Me

Hello! My name is Kate Witham, I am a Political Science major and English minor at UMass Amherst.

## Introduction to Accessibility

The introduction to accessibility secion of this website discusses the importance of considering accessibility while developing web content. It draws on readings from **Laura Kalbag** and **Adobe** to emphasize the importance of accessibility and inclusive design. There are three subsections:

  * What is a Disability?

  * Universal vs. Accessible Design

  * Why does Accessibility Matter?

## Chapter Cards

This section on chapter cards not only explains their role and significance in the world of digital accessibilty, but it also provides **step-by-step walkthroughs** that teach users how to create and edit chapter cards in *ClipChamp*. The subsections are:

  * Introduction to Chapter Cards

  * How to Create Chapter Cards in ClipChamp

  * How to Edit Chapter Cards in ClipChamp
