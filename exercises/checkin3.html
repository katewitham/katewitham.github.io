How to Make Spaghetti Carbonara!

Introduction

Growing up in an Italian household, pasta was a staple of my family's meals. My favorite dish to have was my grandmother's Spaghetti Carbonara. During my time in Italy, I learned how to make it myself and it became one of my most made dishes!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Guanciale
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

Boil a pot of salted water. While the water boils, cut the guanciale in thick peices and cook until crispy. Grate Parmesan cheese. Whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. Once water is boiling, add the spaghetti. Reserve some pasta water before draining the spaghetti. Toss cooked pasta in with guanciale. Remove pan from heat and stir in the egg and cheese mixture, as well as reserved pasta water. Serve immediately and enjoy!
